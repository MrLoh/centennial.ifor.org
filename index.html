Join us to celebrate the 100th birthday of the

About IFOR:

The International Fellowship of Reconciliation (IFOR) was founded in 1914 in response to the horrors of war in Europe. IFOR has taken a consistent stance against war and its preparation throughout its history. Perceiving the need for healing and reconciliation in the world, the founders of IFOR formulated a vision of the human community based upon the belief that love in action has the power to transform unjust political, social, and economic structures.

About the Centennial:

Reflecting on the violence of the past and addressing the challenges of today, approximately 150 delegates from IFOR member organizations across the globe will gather in Konstanz, Germany 100 years after the outbreak of World War I. Worldrenowned nonviolence experts, representatives from like-minded peace organizations, and members of the general public will join IFOR in mourning past violent conflicts, celebrating nonviolent achievements, and envisioning the future.
