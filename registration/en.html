Languages & Interpretation

The event on Friday evening, August 1, will offer simultaneous translation into German, English, French, and Spanish. During the workshops on Saturday, 2 August, there will be whispered and personal translation, for which we need interpreters.


Meals

Lunch and dinner will be served for €6-€9 per meal at the Landratsamt in Konstanz. A snack after the interfaith celebration on Sunday is included in the registration fee for the weekend.

Art Event

On Saturday, Jan Piet van den Berg will create a sculpture in the centre of Konstanz with centenniel participants. Each participant is asked to bring a piece of cloth 100cm x 60cm in size.

Registration Fee

The registration fee for the Centennial weekend (children under 16 are free) is €50 for the whole weekend; for partial participation the fee is €20 for Friday and Saturday each, and €15 for Sunday. This fee includes participation in all programme events, the city tour, the exhibitions, the concert, and the shuttle service to the direct nonviolent action on Friday. Not included in this fee are food, accommodation, and the costs for transportation within Konstanz. Please indicate the registration fee which applies to you and indicate how it will be paid for.



IFOR Council

After the Centennial, the IFOR Council will take place from August 3-9. Only delegates and invited guests can participate in the Council.
