7 - Registration Fee

The registration fee for the Centennial weekend (children under 16 are free) is €50 for the whole weekend; for par- tial participation the fee is €20 for Friday and Saturday each, and €15 for Sunday. This fee includes participation in all programme events, the city tour, the exhibitions, the concert, and the shuttle service to the event on Saturday. Not included in this fee are food, accommodation and the costs for transportation within Konstanz.
